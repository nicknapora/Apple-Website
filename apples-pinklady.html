Pink Lady

This specimen was procured at an orchard near my family's home, alongside the Arkansas Black and Rome Beauty. Of these I ate the Pink Lady last, and I was so relieved to finally enjoy a good apple!

Taste: 4/5

The Pink Lady is an excellent eating apple, similar in flavor profile to the Honeycrisp. While it was mild, being only very slightly tart, the specimen was satisfactory to my palate and would likely be even more favorable to most people.

Texture 5/5

The Pink Lady, pictured right, had by far the best texture of this lineup, and indeed the best of any apple yet reviewed on this site. It was a firm, crisp, eating apple and, of the late season samples used for this website, it was the only one of its kind.

Crispness 5/5

Good texture is strongly related to good crispness, and this Pink Lady specimen corroborated that. While neither its texture nor its crispness were absolutely perfect, they by so far surpass those of the other varieties reviewed that they both easily deserve a five.

Juiciness 4/5

The sample was notably juicy, contributing to its overall strength as an eating apple.
