About This Site

Everyone has preferences in apple varieties, even if they don't realize it. While apple preference can ultimately only be confirmed by trial and error, The Apple Reference is a fantastic place to start. Those aspiring to become discerning apple eaters can do the following here:

  • Peruse reviews of several apple varieties under Apples
  • See how a variety of apples stack up head-to-head on the Leaderboard
  • Learn about the qualities used to assess apples under Metrics
  • Get in touch with an apple expert through Contact
